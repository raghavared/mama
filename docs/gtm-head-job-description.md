# Go-To-Market (GTM) Head - MAMA Project

## About MAMA
MAMA (Marketing Agent Multi-Agent Architecture) is an AI-powered, autonomous marketing automation platform that orchestrates multiple Claude-powered agents to generate, approve, and publish professional marketing content across social media platforms. We're building the future of content marketing — where AI handles the heavy lifting while humans maintain strategic control.

## The Opportunity
We're seeking a strategic GTM Head who can transform our powerful AI content generation engine into a revenue-driving machine. You'll bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and measurable business outcomes, turning content into conversions and engagement into enterprise value.

## The Role

### Core Responsibilities

#### 1. **GTM Strategy & Execution (40%)**
- Define and execute comprehensive go-to-market strategies for AI-generated content across all channels
- Build playbooks for product launches, campaign orchestration, and multi-phase marketing initiatives
- Design audience segmentation strategies leveraging behavioral and psychographic data
- Implement account-based marketing strategies for enterprise targets
- Create frameworks for content → lead → MQL → SQL → revenue attribution

#### 2. **Performance Optimization & Analytics (30%)**
- Build feedback loops between content performance data and AI agent learning
- Implement multi-touch attribution models to track content ROI
- Design A/B testing frameworks for content variants and conversion optimization
- Create predictive models for content performance and audience engagement
- Establish KPIs and success metrics across the entire marketing funnel

#### 3. **Competitive Intelligence & Market Positioning (20%)**
- Integrate real-time competitor monitoring and market trend analysis
- Identify market gaps and emerging content angles
- Build competitive win/loss analysis frameworks
- Feed trending topics and competitive insights into the AI system
- Position MAMA's unique value proposition in the market

#### 4. **Sales Enablement & Channel Integration (10%)**
- Connect marketing content to sales pipeline stages
- Generate sales-ready collateral (battle cards, case studies, one-pagers)
- Build channel-specific strategies (paid, organic, owned media)
- Measure content impact at deal level
- Create feedback loops between sales outcomes and content strategy
